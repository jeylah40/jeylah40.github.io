Our Proccess

Applying to South Harmon is easy. All you need to do is fill out the form below by the admission date. We will then send you two letters. The first will be an acknowledgement that we received your letter. We do this to make sure that you know that we are indeed processing your application and not just putting it aside. The second letter will let you know whether or not you had been accepted to our academy. Below is a list of factors that we take into account when processing your application. None of these are an absolute requirement nor are they an automatic acceptance.

Term Applied For Admission Date

High School GPA

One of the main things we take into account for our incoming freshmen is their high school GPA. We want to make sure that you are serious about your education before coming into our institution. We prefer that our students have at least a 2.5 GPA. We do consider weighted GPAs as we prefer to encourage younger students to take more difficult classes.

Test Score

We require that you have taken either the ACT or the SAT. We then ask that you submit one of the test scores (whichever you prefer) below. The score is provided in the application below but we also require that the score is sent to our admission office for verification. To have the tests automatically sent to us, use our school code: 4815162342

Essay Submission

This is your chance to really shine. We want to get to know who you are through your writing. Your essay should be between 15 and 300,000 words and reflect your true opinions. The current subject for the essay is:

You're going to be stranded on a desert island. What items do you bring with you and why?
